# Claim Boundaries

ClaimBound Public Benchmarks publishes narrow evidence records. A result can be
positive, negative, blocked by source access, or not yet reproduced.

## Allowed Current Claim

```text
NASA POWER D-103 passed the pre-registered gate under protocol 1.0.143 and was
independently reproduced at outcome/gate level on 2026-04-29.
```

This claim is limited to the documented source, points, period, target,
candidate, controls and acceptance gate.

## Forbidden Claims

Do not claim:

- raw payload hash reproduced exactly;
- universal forecasting edge;
- public-health alert readiness;
- energy-system deployment readiness;
- superiority over all statistical methods;
- private-background performance;
- machine-learning breakthrough.

## Negative And Blocked Results

Negative and blocked results are valid outputs. They should be recorded with the
same level of source lineage, command history and claim boundaries as positive
results.

## Reproduction Levels

Use exact language:

- `not independently reproduced`;
- `reproduced at outcome/gate level`;
- `reproduced at outcome/gate level with source-byte drift`;
- `raw-byte reproduction`, only if the raw payload hashes match exactly.
